Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 15, 2025

Summary: Odak is designed with privacy in mind. All your data stays on your device. We don't collect, store, or share any personal information.

Information We Collect

Odak does not collect any personal information. The app operates entirely on your device:

  • All event data (countdowns, milestones) is stored locally on your device
  • Photos you add to widgets remain on your device
  • No data is transmitted to external servers
  • No analytics or tracking is implemented

Data Storage

All data created within Odak is stored locally on your iOS device using secure system storage. This includes:

  • Event names and dates
  • Widget configurations
  • Custom background images

This data is not accessible to us or any third parties.

Third-Party Services

Odak does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The app functions completely offline.

Data Sharing

We do not share any data with third parties because we do not collect any data. Your information never leaves your device.

Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control over it:

  • You can delete any event at any time within the app
  • Uninstalling the app removes all associated data
  • You can export or share your data using iOS system features

Children's Privacy

Odak does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
